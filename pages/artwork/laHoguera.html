La Hoguera

// Performance

You come to La Hoguera and you see a piece of contemporary theater that could be something else. It could be a ritual gathering, but it's not; it could be a tablao, but it's not; it could be an ecstatic, choreographed dance, but it's not; it could be a sports competition, but it's not. It is still a piece of theater that makes you remember those who are no longer there, the people you have lost and those you have expelled for a silence that has become a flag and a competition.

La Hoguera reflects on the forms of connection and care between beings in a masculine society that is increasingly fragmented and isolated.

Be patient.

Creation, Direction and Performance: Alberto Escartí, Xavier Giménez
Music: Carlos Hermoso de Mendoza
