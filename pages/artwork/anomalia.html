a n o m a l i a

// Audiovisual and physical performance

"An error reveals not only a failure of the system, but also its operational logic".
(Nunes, Mark. "Failure Notice")

"a n o m a l i a " is a sound, visual and physical performance focused on the exploration of error as a generative phenomenon, capable of creating new paths and perceptions about the systems that surround us. It bases its dramaturgy and methodology on the adaptability of the human towards the uncontrollability of the machine.

The project stems from the need to listen to and re-signify the error, removing it from its negative imaginary. It takes as its core element the scenic dialogue between the digital and the physical, generating new sonic, visual and physical languages based on probability, noise and the body.

Sound: Marco Ferreira
Visuals and Body: Jana Tox
