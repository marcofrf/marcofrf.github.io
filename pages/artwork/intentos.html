Intentos

// Live Event Production

A project that is not a project.

An attempt to highlight the commonalities that emerge from the intersection of different artistic expressions, creating a unique narrative. INTENTOS' aims to increase and diversify the quantity and variety of stimuli to which a performance exposes us. It therefore chooses to explore the current artistic panorama of the city of Oporto, with a specific aesthetic branch as its pillar, uniting the different elements that make up the experience.

In this first edition, INTENTOS brings together Porto artists from the fields of theatre and music to create a dark, experimental and raw aesthetic environment. The three different projects therefore present the same character as a common point of work and present Espaço MIRA as the host of this programme.

Artists: Carina Ferrão, Sara Neves, Francisco Oliveira, Blanc Motif

Production: Marco Ferreira
Technical Director: Marco Ferreira
