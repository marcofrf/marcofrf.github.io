modul

// Cultural Platform

modul is a cultural platform dedicated to the organization of events revolving around media culture, in Valencia.

The platform has two main objectives: firstly, to present new ideas and methodologies, from the approach of the public to the processes of creation, experimentation and performance related to digital practices.
The second is to create a fruitful network of artists, where different interactions and projects can be developed.
