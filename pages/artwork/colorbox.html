ColorBox

// VR musical interface built with Unity3D

Bridging digital processing and analogue intuition.

ColorBox is a VR sound interface that aims to bring the user closer to exploring new musical performance gestures. Having the standardisation of digital controllers in mind, the application provides a pseudo-tangible interactive environment, a virtual space in which to work with sound based on the body and movement. The instrument consists of positioning and manipulating small cubes on different lasers to generate, process and mix different sounds.

Sound and Visuals: Marco Ferreira
Interaction Designer and Programmer: Marco Ferreira
