Ignis Fatuus

// Live AV with VR

Ignis Fatuus is an audiovisual performance with virtual reality modeling that was presented on December 7th at Pluto, in Valencia.

The piece concept is centered around the journey and salvation of wandering souls, here represented as watery blue flames. It speaks about the relation between life, death and the limbo in between.

The aesthetics of real time VR sculptures functioned as the starting point of the entire audiovisual composition. The sculpting processed was streamed using NDI and processed in Touchdesigner, alongside data obtained from a Kinect device that tracked the movements of the VR artist.

VR Modeling: Manel Bafaluy
Visuals and Interaction Design: Marco Ferreira
Music and Sound Design: eddgem
